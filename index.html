About The Beauty Hub

The Beauty Hub is a modern beauty and lifestyle brand dedicated to helping women look and feel their best. We specialize in professional hair services, makeup, and trendy sneakers — all in one place.

Founded with a passion for style and confidence, our goal is to provide high-quality, affordable beauty experiences that leave every client feeling empowered and confident.

Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or just want to upgrade your everyday look, The Beauty Hub is here to bring out your best version.

Booking Policy

To ensure a smooth and professional service experience at The Beauty Hub, please take note of our booking terms below:

  • 💳 A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required to secure all bookings.
  • ⏰ Arrive on time for your appointment. Clients arriving more than 30 minutes late without communication may need to reschedule, and this will be treated as a cancellation.
  • ❌ Cancellations or rescheduling must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in loss of deposit.
  • 📅 Same-day bookings are subject to availability and confirmation.
  • 💄 Please arrive with clean lace and plaited hair flat enough for a flawless install. Extra charges apply for lace cleaning or hair washing.
  • 📲 Bookings are confirmed via WhatsApp (0684123632) or email only.

Thank you for respecting our time and helping us serve you better 💕 Your support means the world, and we look forward to making you feel confident and beautiful.
